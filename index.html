Process

How to Use Your Benefits

Follow these steps to certify your education benefits at SHSU each semester.

01
📋

Determine Your Benefit

Find out which VA chapter or state benefit applies to you — GI Bill®, Hazlewood Act, Tuition Assistance, or another program. Visit VA.gov or contact our office.

02
📄

Gather Your Documents

Obtain your Certificate of Eligibility (COE), DD-214, or authorization forms from your branch or the VA. First-time users only need to submit the COE once.

03
🖥️

Submit Online

Log in to the MySam Portal and upload all required documents through our secure certification request system. No paper, fax, or email submissions accepted.

04
✅

Get Certified

Our team certifies your enrollment with the VA or TVC. You'll receive timely updates to ensure you meet all deadlines and requirements each term.

Programs

Benefits We Process

We support a wide range of VA and state educational benefit programs for military-affiliated students.

🎓

GI Bill® — Chapter 33 (Post-9/11)

Covers tuition and fees paid directly to SHSU, plus a monthly housing allowance (BAH) and a books/supplies stipend — based on your percentage of eligibility.

Tuition Direct Pay Monthly BAH Book Stipend Learn more →
⭐

Hazlewood Act (Texas)

Texas honors its veterans with up to 150 tuition-exempt credit hours for eligible veterans who entered service with Texas as their home of record or place of entry.

150 Credit Hours Texas Veterans Legacy Option Check eligibility →
🪖

Chapter 31 — VR&E

Veteran Readiness & Employment pays tuition and fees directly via Purchase Order and may include a book stipend for eligible undergraduates. Work with your VRE counselor.

Direct Pay Books Covered Learn more →
🏅

Military Tuition Assistance

Each military branch administers TA differently. Active-duty students must get ESO approval before enrolling. Air Force uses AFVEC; Army uses ArmyIgnitED.

Active Duty Branch Specific Get guidance →

Other VA Chapters

We also certify Chapter 30 (Montgomery GI Bill), Chapter 35 (Survivors & Dependents), and Chapter 1606 (Selected Reserve). Contact us to confirm your chapter.
